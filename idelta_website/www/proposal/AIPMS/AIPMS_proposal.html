Section A: Executive Summary

Inspired Delta Ltd submits this proposal for the supply, configuration, hosting, and support of the Addressing Infrastructure Programme Management System (AIPMS). Our recommended delivery model is a hybrid implementation built on ERPNext and the Frappe Framework, with targeted custom modules for the AIPMS Configuration Panel, Field Data Tool (FDT) integration, programme-specific workflows, and reporting requirements.

The solution is designed to meet the RFP objective of delivering a rapid, operational programme platform without taking on the cost and schedule risk of a full bespoke build. We will use proven ERPNext capabilities for contracts, HR, payroll, document management, workflow, dashboards, and auditability, then extend them where the RFP requires programme-specific controls such as selective vendor workflows, payment certification logic, field-level HR restrictions, and FDT import validation.

Rapid Delivery

Lite Version for Modules 1-5 by end of Week 2, ahead of the Week 3 hard deadline.

Open Platform

ERPNext/Frappe foundation with open exports, transparent configuration, and continuity beyond a single vendor.

Programme Control

Section C: Technical Approach

Proposed Delivery Model

Inspired Delta Ltd proposes a hybrid AIPMS implementation: ERPNext provides the base platform for projects, workflows, documents, procurement, HR, payroll, and reporting; custom Frappe components provide the AIPMS-specific Configuration Panel, payment certification workflows, FDT ingestion, additional dashboards, and field-level governance controls. This is the fastest route to operational readiness while preserving flexibility for future programme changes.

Configuration Panel Design

The AIPMS Configuration Panel will be implemented as administrator-controlled DocTypes and forms within Frappe. Tier 1 users will be able to maintain contract values, cost lines, working capital advance settings, unit payment rates, zone targets, milestone percentages, retention rules, organisational roles, escalation thresholds, and report parameters without direct code edits. Changes will be permission-controlled and audit-logged.

Detailed Module Delivery Notes

Module Detailed Implementation Approach
Module 1: Milestone & Programme Schedule Programme schedules will be configured through ERPNext Projects, Tasks, Task Types, and Project Templates, with milestone sign-off workflows, real-time progress views, deadline alerts, and milestone status reports generated from live transactional data.
Module 2: Contract & Vendor Management Contracts will be managed through a structured digital register covering parties, value, dates, amendments, and workflow status. The Selective Vendor Register will be implemented with controlled nomination-to-approval routing, categorisation, escalation handling, and variation-control approval paths.
Module 3: Payment Certification & Drawdown Custom payment DocTypes will manage working capital advances, drawdown claims, milestone-linked payment logic, payment certificate generation, retention calculations, and reconciliation reporting. FDT imports will be linked to payment validation and discrepancy-hold workflows.
Module 4: Document Management & Audit Trail Documents will be attached to programme records with version tracking, mandatory-attachment gates for payment and approval steps, approval-state locking to prevent unauthorised edits, and audit visibility over document actions and record changes.
Module 5: Governance & Meeting Management Meeting records will cover agenda, attendees, minutes, decisions, quorum rules, extraordinary sessions, and follow-up actions. Governance approvals and milestone sign-offs will be linked to downstream payment and reporting processes.
Module 6: Reporting & Dashboards Dashboards will provide governance, PMO, financial, HR, escalation, and FDT import visibility. Reports will include commitment and variance reporting, workstream progress, payment summaries, import logs, and export-ready outputs in open formats.
Module 7: Human Resource Management ERPNext HR/Payroll will be configured for employee files, contracts, attendance, deployment, payroll calculation, statutory deductions, training records, and bulk CSV onboarding. Additional controls will handle deployment-linked remuneration and restricted access to salary and bank data.

Technical Requirements Mapping

Hosting & Infrastructure
  • Cloud-hosted production environment with declared data residency in the final hosting schedule.
  • 99.5% monthly uptime SLA minimum, excluding agreed maintenance windows.
  • Daily backups with point-in-time recovery design targeting RPO of 1 hour and RTO of 4 hours.
  • Architecture sized for at least 50 concurrent users, with scaling options during peak milestone periods.
Security & Access Control
  • TLS in transit and encrypted storage for production data and protected backups.
  • 2FA enforced for Tier 1-4 users and configurable for other tiers.
  • RBAC at module, record, and field level, especially for HR/payroll data.
  • Audit logging for create, update, approval, export, and privileged access events.
  • Pre-go-live penetration testing with remediation of critical findings before production sign-off.
FDT Integration Module
  • Phase 1 CSV import with schema validation, duplicate detection, field-level error reporting, and immutable batch logs.
  • Imported FDT records retained as source records; payment discrepancies flagged for manual review rather than overwritten.
  • Phase 2 API option can be provided as an optional enhancement, subject to the final FDT interface specification.
Offline & Mobile
  • Web access across current desktop and mobile browsers.
  • Offline-first attendance and deployment capture for designated HR field forms, with background sync after reconnection.
  • Scope includes configuration, testing, and user training for low-connectivity operating conditions.
